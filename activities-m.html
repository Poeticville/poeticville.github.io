CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Many Co-Curricular activities are organized by the school, which is indicative of our committment to an holistic training for our students, in order to produce an all-round balanced individual. These activities can be grouped under:

  Sport (Outdoor and Indoor)
  Games
  Clubs
  Societies
  Events and Festivities

Sports (Outdoor and Indoor)

"Healthy Body, Healthy Mind". At HAGCO, we are well aware of the therapeutic effects of a healthy body to the mind, and how much athletics and sports contribute to this. A healthy and sound mind remains one of our chief goals in the overall development of your child/ward, which is why we have designed a rich Physical Education curriculum for our students. Apart from our Inter-House Sports, which holds every third (3rd) term of a session, our students do engage in leisure sporting games like football (soccer), volleyball, handball and badmington.

Interhouse Sports - Sack Race


Games

In line with our committment to the mental development of your child/ward, we encourage them to participate in indoor non-physical games like Scrabble, Chess and Snakes & Ladder.
