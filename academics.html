ACADEMIC CURRICULUM


HAGCO runs the Junior Secondary School and Senior Secondary School Programmes as specified in the Nigerian National Policy on Education. Being a very young school (Est. 2015), we only have for now, Junior School students who will advance into the Senior school come next academic session (2018/19). They have written their first Basic Education Certificate Examination. We offer a wide spectrum of subjects taught by qualified and dedicated staff.


JUNIOR SCHOOL CURRICULUM

14 subjects are offered at the Junior Secondary level namely:

  • English Studies (Core Subject)
  • Mathematics (Core Subject)
  • French Language
  • Tourism
  • Social Studies
  • Business Studies
  • Basic Technology
  • Basic Science
  • Home Economics
  • Civic Education
  • Agricultural Science
  • Cultural and Creative Art
  • Christian Religious Studies
  • Physical and Health Education
The pass mark for promotion to the next class is 60% in the eight (8) core subjects. Assessment is not only in the area of intellectual ability but also in attitudinal (affective) and psychomotor (manipulative) areas of learning.


SENIOR SCHOOL CURRICULUM

These 17 subjects will be offered at the Senior Secondary level.

  • English Studies (Core Subject)
  • Mathematics (Core Subject)
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Geography
  • Commerce
  • Government
  • Further Mathematics
  • Accounting
  • Economics
  • Agricultural Science
  • Literature in English
  • Christian Religious Studies
  • Health Science
  • Computer Studies
  • Food and Nutrition

At the end of their studies, the students will sit for the Cambridge A-Level Examination.
