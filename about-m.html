FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S DESK

Handmaids Girls College (HAGCO) Obudu, is a Catholic school founded by the Handmaids of the Holy Child Jesus (HHCJ) South Eastern Province and established on the 28th of September 2015. The purpose of our institution is to educate the girl-child and to empower her to fully develop her potentials. Thus the education we offer the girl-child is holistic, and the training enables her to develop her intellectual ability while building her up in good moral standing as well as spiritual, physical and social development. Since the school cannot achieve this goal alone, we therefore need the co-operation of the child herself and her parents. She will practicalise the act of self giving through her readiness and willingness to study hard in order to be useful to others in the community within the school and outside the school. This will help her to avoid self-centredness and selfishness; she is educated in acts of charity and services to the poor, our Motto: "Love & Service".

As a Catholic School we believe in providing an opportunity for sound moral and intellectual development of the youth especially the girl child for a successful career, self actualization and service to humanity. Generally, life consists of many elements contributing to a positive living. Our handbook contains the school’s rules and regulations. It seeks to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the school policies, services and organization that HAGCO offers you.

Read it, keep it handy for reference and if lost you have to purchase another copy. It is very important for us that your stay be as comfortable and experiential as possible. It is our unspoken goal to exceed your expectations. We take great pride in providing our students with the highest levels of professional services. We will challenge and guide them to think and discover their unique talents. Capitalize on every opportunity and do all that lies within your power to make your school a better place to live and learn. Scholarship will be given, but beneficiaries will be students with proven academic ability. At the end of their studies here at HAGCO, they will seat for the Cambridge A-Levels Exams.

Although academic work remains the bedrock of our school programme, much learning takes place outside the classroom. Your years here will prepare you for life but they will also be a time of great personal growth, challenges and real happiness. The knowledge and friends you get and make at HAGCO can be your life time opportunity. The entire Handmaids Community hopes that your experience in HAGCO proves worthwhile for your entire life.

Sr. Anthonia Eze
 Principal

PURPOSE FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT

The Congregation of the Handmaids of the Holy Child Jesus (HHCJ) ministers to the people of God through education at all levels with special attention to children in general, the Girl – child in particular, and women empowerment. The establishment of the Handmaids Girls College Obudu came as a result of the consistent demand from parents for continuity of the holistic training their children receive from Handmaids International Nursery/Primary School Obudu.

The sisters have also observed that the catholic missionary activities in terms of Education at the Secondary level is not very prominent in Obudu and Obanliku Areas. Most parents who seek boarding for their Children at this level have to travel to Ogoja and beyond. It is to this end that the congregation of the Handmaids of the Holy Child Jesus felt the need for the establishment of this institution here in Obudu.


OUR MISSION

Handmaids Schools, grounded in our motto of "Love & Service" provide students an opportunity for an authentic holistic Christian growth in a life illuminated by the Gospel message and total love and service to GOD and humanity.


OUR VISION

To produce God – fearing individuals, who will through their lives, contribute towards building a just and responsible society.


OUR SPIRITUALITY

Christian truths and values will be integrated into the student's total development. All students therefore, shall:

  • Learn to revere all persons as children of God and to respect them and all creation
  • Internalize Christian beliefs through participation in liturgical celebrations, as well as personal and public prayers.
  • Be of service to the church, family, school and the civic community
  • Begin the process of discernment regarding their vocation in life


OUR PROMISES

These are our promises to you should you decide to send your child/ward to HAGCO:

  • An Holistic Education for her
  • A good moral upbringing
  • Her spirituality will be enkindled and built up in the Holy Catholic Faith
  • She will have a healthy sense of self-worth
  • She will be taught self-reliance
  • Empathy for others will be inculcated in her
  • She will be taught charity; to care for the poor and needy
  • With her co-operation, she will achieve Academic excellence
  • At the end of her studies at HAGCO, she will sit for the Cambridge A-Levels Examination
  • Scholarship opportunities for further studies overseas
