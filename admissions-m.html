ADMISSION


GENERAL INFORMATION

HAGCO is a Catholic Boarding School for young girls who have:

  Attained the age of 10
  Completed primary six
  Written the first school leaving certificate
      examinations
  Passed the entrance examination
  Been interviewed by the school

A candidate loses her chance of admission if she fails to report to school or pay the required fees as at when due. No student will be admitted into any class other than class one (JS 1 or SS 1).

By right of admission, students are strictly bound to live up to expectations of the school rules or be adequately disciplined if they default.
