SECURITY


The safety of your child/ward is foremost to us. That is why we have men well trained and experienced in security to see to this. They run in shifts: Day and Night, and are only entitled for breaks during their OFF days (in which provision for their substitution is made) and when the students are on holidays.


STAFF


The College has 19 Academic staff who are qualified and dedicated to teaching. The minimum qualification for employment is a Bachelorâ€™s degree in Education. To avoid underutilization of their training, we make it compulsory that each teacher be employed in his/her area of specialization. The college organises workshops and seminars every term for our staff, so as to improve them, and hence deliver better and up-to-date knowledge to your child/ward.


Auxillary (Non-Academic) Staff

The auxiliary staff are also employed based on their qualification for the different posts they occupy. Secretaries, Receptionist and Librarians must have at least a Diploma Certificate. Gardeners, Farm managers, Cooks, Cleaners, Refectorians and Security personnels must possess at least a Senior School Certificate (SSCE).


FACILITIES


HAGCO has all it takes to give your child/ward a quality education. Modern facilities like a well-stocked library, a functional dormitory, a working chemistry laboratory, spacious classrooms, fields for games and ICT LAB for computer studies are put in place for effective teaching and learning. As a boarding school, your child/ward will have enough time for study, co-curricular and spiritual activities, socializing with peers and making friends within and outside the country.
